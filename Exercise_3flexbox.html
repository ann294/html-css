Types of Cakes

Pound Cake

traditional cake variety made from 4 ingredients - flour, eggs, butter and sugar. These cakes are free from frosting or decoration, but some bakers prefer dusting it with powdered sugar or garnishing them with nuts.

Red-velvet Cakes

name Red Velvet from the reddish color and the velvety texture. The cake tastes more like a chocolate cake, but the cream cheese frosting makes the differentiating factor.

Layer Cake

contains multiple layers of cake glued together with frosting or filling. A sponge or a butter cake gets sliced into multiple layers and re-assembled by adding fruit compotes or jams

Upside-down Cake

called the “Upside Down” cake because the toppings are on the bottom and the sponge on top. baked in a single pan by pouring batter over caramelised fruits like pineapple, apple etc

Cheese Cakes

uses soft cheese like Ricotta as its main ingredient. Cheese gets combined with eggs and sugar to form the main element of the cake. The crust base or the bottom layer can be crushed graham crackers, biscuits or even a thin sponge.

Genoise Cake

cake is a type of Italian sponge cake with origins in Genoa, Italy preparation process involves using the whole egg instead of seperating the yolk and whites .The eggs are whisked with sugar, flour and melted butter before spreading the batter on a baking sheet.
